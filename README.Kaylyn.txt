Title: Stocks (Hard Solution)

Overview
This spreadsheet contains three years of stock data (2018,2019, and 2020)
Program will pull following information in top right
•	Greatest % increase
•	Greatest % decrease
•	Greatest total Volume

Conditional formatting will show green where a ticker has increased in value,
And red where a ticker has decreased value.
